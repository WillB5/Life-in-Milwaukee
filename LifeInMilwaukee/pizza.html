Pizza Places

=Milwaukee has its fair share of pizza places, some of my favorites are:

  1. Jet's Pizza
  2. Classic Slice
  3. Sal's Pizza

Jet's Pizza sells a very tasty detroit-style pizza, which is a deep dish pizza similar to Rocky Roccoco pizza.

Classic Slice and Sal's Pizza are both pizza places that are known as a "by the slice" pizza place, however, you can still order whole pizzas.

Visit Jet's Pizza
- Popular Detroit-Style pizza place
Visit Classic Slice
- Modern pizza by the slice place
Visit Sal's Pizza
