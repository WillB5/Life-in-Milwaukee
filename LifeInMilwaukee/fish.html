Fish in Milwaukee

Fish in Milwaukee are plentiful, with beautiful locations to go fishing. Being next to Lake Michigan makes it very easy to find a spot to look for fish.

Here are some of the fish you can find in Milwaukee:

  • Brown Trout
  • Flathead Catfish
  • Walleye

A full list of the 54 types of fish in Milwaukee can be found on Milwaukee Riverkeeper.
