Ice Cream

Since Wisconsin is known as the dairy state, we have lots of tasty dairy products, one of them being ice cream.

Here are some of the top ice cream places in Milwaukee:

  1. Kopps's Frozen Custard
  2. Leon's Frozen Custard
  3. The Purple Door

Vanilla custard has a slightly different taste from regular vanilla ice cream, however, it is definitely worth a try if you haven't had custard before.
